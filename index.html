Authority Portal

Government monitoring dashboard with AI-powered fraud detection, real-time shop analytics, and interactive maps.

  • AI fraud analyst chatbot

  • Transaction audit trail

Customer Portal

Beneficiary-facing portal for viewing ration allocations, booking queue slots, and tracking live stock availability.

  • Monthly allocation overview
  • Digital queue & QR token booking
  • Live stock availability tracker
  • Feedback & rating system
Beneficiary access

Shop Owner Portal

FPS operator dashboard for managing real-time queue, live inventory data, and daily ration dispensation operations.

  • Live queue management & token system
  • Real-time Firebase inventory sync
  • Dispensation flow chart
  • Live activity feed
FPS operator access
