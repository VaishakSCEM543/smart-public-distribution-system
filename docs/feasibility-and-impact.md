# Feasibility & Impact – Smart PDS

## Technical Feasibility

- Built using low-cost, widely available components
- Uses open-source libraries
- Scalable cloud backend
- Compatible with existing ration shop infrastructure

---

## Economic Feasibility

- Reduces leakage losses (20–30% estimated nationally)
- Minimal additional hardware cost
- Long-term savings through fraud prevention

---

## Social Impact

- Ensures fair distribution
- Reduces beneficiary exploitation
- Increases trust in welfare systems
- Reduces waiting time and disputes

---

## Scalability

- Deployable across multiple shops
- Centralized monitoring possible at district/state level
